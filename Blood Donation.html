ABOUT US

Global Blood Donation is an organisation that is geared to support hospitals and charitable organizations by connecting them to the donors and the respective companies when needed.

Individual volunteers are required to use this platform to render their donation details that will narrow down the blood donors search during emergencies. This helps to reduce the number of deaths of the patients in various countries as result of saving a lot of lives.

The Global Blood Donation has registered with wide range of hospitals globally for volunteers to conduct their test freely and have their test results verified before any contact.

Our impact goes beyond moving money to where it's needed the most; it's also about putting communableseat of change and shifting power to transform the way aid and philanthropy work.
